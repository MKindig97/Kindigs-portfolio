I believe the most important element to any successful team is a balanced individual, here are a few of the actvities which keep me busy day to day.

Guitar Playing

When I'm not busy building great programs with my team, I'm hard at work shredding some spicy licks on my good ol stratocaster. Playing guitar for me is a great way to decompress; whilst at the same time being an awesome way of keeping your mind and fingers strong!

I started playing guitar 7 years ago and I haven't put it down since! All it took for me to pick up my first axe was to hear my heroes on the radio in thier full glory. Every chord and every melody sung to my soul.

Once I gathered the courage to pick up the guitar and play, it wasen't long until I found a group of guys who shared the same passion as me. Even if they did not play the same instrument as me, I felt a strong bond for my new bandmates.

These individuals gave me the reassurance that as long as you work together, understand the value of teamwork, you'll always know the feeling of accomplishment.

Skiing

Even though I went skiing for my first time this past year, I think I've found one of my new favorite hobbies! Not only is skiing a great way to stay in shape, but it also offers a diffrent perspective on what humans are capable of.

Anyone that has ever scaled a mountain will tell you that there is no greater feeling than conquering those 14er's. But, imagine adding a rollercoaster ride at the end of that experince. Thats basically skiing!

Doing larger than life activites, such as skiing, gives people the confidence to overcome their mental fears by facing them head on physically.

As a team leader, it is important to be mindful of challenges or fears you may face and tackle them head on, like going down a double black diamond at 30 mph.

E-Commerce

Ever since I got out of highscool, it was always my dream to be an entrepenaur. The only problem was, I didn't have the knowledge, capital or understanding of micro and macro-econmics to get the ball rolling.

It wasn't until recently I discovered the power of E-Commerce; selling of any retail products online. Not only did this give me the experince of watching a business run in real time, but also gave me a better understanding of my customers needs.

I started small with only a few dollars in my bank account and turned that into a sustaianable system where I can provide my customers an enjoyable shopping experince with selection, and of course making some passive income on the side.

Even though this is a new hobbie for me, I know it will become a lifelong venture. When it comes to building up a successful team environement, it is important to ensure one is a trailblazer and doesn't wait for someone to give the first move; take action!
